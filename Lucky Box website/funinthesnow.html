Fun in the snow

If one thinks of the winter season fun, one might think of walking in the snow. You don't have the leaves of summer getting in your way and you get an entirely different landscape.

Be careful of snowy mountains

Before beginning a hike, in case of emergencies it's always a good idea to have a hike plan written out

Be careful of snow fields

These areas can have dips you can't see while walking; especially beside small rivers.

Loads of things can happen in the snow, best keep a look out
